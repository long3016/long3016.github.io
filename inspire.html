About

As an artists, I often gravitate towards others especially those that radiate passion for their work. One of my inspirations is John Powell, a musical composer for several well known works, such as How To Train Your Dragon, Solo: A Star Wars Story, and Rio.

His work is so wildly inspirational that his 2010 score for the film How To Train Your Dragon earned his first Academy Award nomination at the 83rd Axademy Award Ceremony!

John Powell inspires me to learn from others, for years he started his career as an assistant to Patrick Doyle, a famous Scottish Composer.
By making those connections and learning from others, his work is ever growing while still inspiring others to create and find their passion for art.
