Give Yourself Permission to be Creative

"That's when art's not a luxury, it's a sustenance. We need it." Ethan Hawke

Reflecting on moments that shaped his life, actor Ethan Hawke examines how courageous expression promotes healing and connection with one another -- and invites you to discover your own unbashed creativity. "There is no path till you walk it, you have to be willing to play the fool." he says.
