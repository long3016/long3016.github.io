Midly Amusing

By Payton Long

Pointer Pointer

All that have to do is load the website and move your mouse pointer. Wherever you move the pointer, the website will automatically generate an image of a person pointing at it. Don't believe me? I've sat for minutes at a time trying to find a spot it can't point to.

Pointer Pointer

Endless Horse

Out of all the websites I have visited, this one is by far the longest one. No matter how far you scroll down, the horse is never ending. He just keeps growing.

Endless Horse

Cat Bounce

Just like the name suggest, this site is all about cats bouncing. Allowing you to move the cats around and bounce across the screen, it is a great mindless activity. Plus with an extra surprise when you hit Make It Rain.

Cat Bounce
